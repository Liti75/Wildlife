Mrugavani National Park

Location & Area: Located near Chilkur, Hyderabad, Telangana. Spans ~850 acres (3.4 km²). Declared a national park in 1994.

Geography & Habitat

Situated in the Deccan Plateau region with undulating terrain and hillocks. A mix of rocky and grassland zones with semi-evergreen forest.

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Indian hare, mongoose, monitor lizard, civet, porcupines, and over 100 bird species including peacocks and warblers.
  • Flora: Bamboo, teak, sandalwood, palas, amla, and various medicinal plants.

Activities

  • Forest walk trails and jungle safaris
  • Environmental education and library
  • View tower and auditorium shows

Accessibility & Permits

Located 25 km from Hyderabad city center. Open to visitors with entry fee. Accessible via road and local buses.

Significance

Popular weekend eco-tourism spot near Hyderabad; contributes to biodiversity preservation and public awareness.
