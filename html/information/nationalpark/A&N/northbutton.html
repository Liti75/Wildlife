North Button Island National Park

Location & Area: Situated in Baratang, North Andaman (~60 km from Long Island), it covers just ~0.44 km². A marine gem established in 1979.

Ecology & Highlights

Covered by moist tropical forest, it’s surrounded by deep blue waters teeming with dolphins, dugongs, spotted deer, monitor lizards, and turtles. The best time to visit: December–April.

Activities

  • Scuba-diving & snorkeling over coral reefs
  • Boat safaris to spot dolphins and dugongs
  • Guided wildlife and bird-watching walks

Access & Logistics

Reachable by boat via Rangat and Long Island. Accommodation options are limited to nearby islands and eco-resorts.
