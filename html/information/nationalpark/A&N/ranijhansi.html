Rani Jhansi Marine National Park

Location & Area: Established in 1996, this archipelago park spans ~256 km² of land and marine area, in Ritchie's Archipelago (~30 km from Port Blair).

Geography & Ecosystems

Encompassing John Lawrence, Henry Lawrence, and Outram Islands, the park features mangroves, semi-evergreen and lowland rainforest, beaches, lagoons, and vibrant coral reefs.

Flora & Fauna

  • Marine creatures: dugongs, saltwater crocodiles, sea turtles
  • Corals & reef fish diversity
  • Fruit‑eating bats (key pollinators), white‑bellied sea eagles.

Activities

  • Scuba‑diving & snorkeling in coral-rich waters
  • Bird‑watching and mangrove exploration
  • Boat tours and wildlife photo‑safaris

Access & Management

Accessible by boat from Port Blair via Ritchie's Archipelago. It's protected under IUCN Category II and managed by the A&N Forest Department.
