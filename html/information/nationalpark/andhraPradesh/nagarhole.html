Nagarhole National Park

Location & Area: Located in Kodagu & Mysore, Karnataka; area ~642 km². Established 1988, declared Tiger Reserve 1999, part of Nilgiri Biosphere.

Geography & Habitat

Northwestern Ghats moist deciduous forest (teak, rosewood) and dry deciduous scrub; features rivers, streams and waterfalls.

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Bengal tigers, Indian elephants, leopards, gaurs, chital, sambar deer, dholes; designated under Project Tiger & Elephant.
  • Flora: Teak, rosewood, sandalwood, silver oak; sub-montane swamps with Eugenia spp.

Activities

  • Jeep safaris for tiger and elephant sightings
  • Boat rides in Kabini reservoir
  • Birdwatching – hornbills, eagles, etc.

Accessibility & Permits

Accessible from Mysore (94 km); park entry via Kabini or Dhikala gates; permits/ferry booked via forest dept.

Significance

Key conservation area in Western Ghats; nominated for UNESCO WHS; part of Nilgiri Biosphere.
