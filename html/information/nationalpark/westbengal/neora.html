Neora Valley National Park

Location & Area: Located in Kalimpong district, West Bengal; extends across West Bengal–Sikkim–Bhutan trijunction; area approx. 88 km²; established in 1986 :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Geography & Habitat

Elevation ranges from ~183 m to ~3,200 m (Rachela Danda peak). The terrain consists of virgin montane forests, bamboo groves, rhododendron thickets, cascading streams, and wetlands like Panchpokhri and Jorepokhri .

Flora & Fauna

  • Flora: Ecosystems include subtropical mixed broadleaf, lower temperate evergreen, upper temperate broadleaf and rhododendron forests. Notable plant species include Rhododendron arboreum, R. falconeri, bamboo, oak, sal, ferns, and ~52 orchid species including endemic ones.
  • Fauna: Home to rare mammals such as Red Panda, Himalayan Tahr, Clouded Leopard, Asiatic Black Bear, Sambar, Barking Deer, Serow, Goral, Dhole, Gaur, Leopard, Leopard Cat, Golden Cat, Sloth Bear, Himalayan Flying Squirrel, and occasionally Royal Bengal Tiger .
  • Birdlife & Others: Over 258–308 species of birds recorded, including satyr tragopan, rufous‑throated partridge, crimson‑breasted woodpecker, bay woodpecker, golden‑throated barbet, and vulnerable rungous‑necked hornbill. Reptiles such as king cobra, pit viper, blind snake and diverse insects contribute to the biodiversity .

Activities & Trekking Routes

  • Trekking routes include Lava–Alubari–Rechela Danda, offering panoramic views of Kanchenjunga and dense forests.
  • Guided nature walks, birdwatching trips, and visits to nature interpretation center at Lava. Camping and cultural encounters organized in forest lodges/campsites .

Accessibility & Best Time

Nearest airport: Bagdogra (~100 km from Lava); nearest railheads: Darjeeling (narrow gauge, ~30 km) and New Jalpaiguri (~132 km). Road access via Siliguri–Kalimpong–Lava/Samsing routes; permits issued at Lava and Samsing forest offices . Best visiting season: October to April; monsoon closure typically June–September; birdwatching peak December to early spring.
