Khalasuni Wildlife Sanctuary

Location & Area: Sambalpur District, Odisha; part of Sambalpur Elephant Reserve, adjacent to Badrama Sanctuary. Area not officially documented on Wikipedia :contentReference[oaicite:20]{index=20}.

Geography & Habitat

Forested corridor connecting Satkosia–Badrama landscapes; habitat includes dry deciduous forest and elephant corridor zones :contentReference[oaicite:21]{index=21}.

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Asian elephant, leopard, wild dog, civet, peafowl, hornbill, junglefowl and associated medium mammals :contentReference[oaicite:22]{index=22}.
  • Flora: Typical dry deciduous canopy with sal, acacias, bamboo and understory shrubs (unverified details) :contentReference[oaicite:23]{index=23}.

Activities

  • Mostly conservation‑use for wildlife migration and ecological monitoring—no public tourism facilities documented.

Accessibility & Permits

Access regulated by Odisha Forest Department; used primarily for elephant corridor protection efforts.

Significance

Critical link within Sambalpur elephant reserve, supporting movement of elephants and biodiversity between contiguous protected areas :contentReference[oaicite:24]{index=24}.
