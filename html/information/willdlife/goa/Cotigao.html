Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary

Location & Area: Established in 1968, located in Canacona taluka (South Goa); area ~87 km². Features eco‑tourism centre and treetop watchtower. :contentReference[oaicite:16]{index=16}

Geography & Habitat

Tall moist deciduous, semi-evergreen and evergreen forests with clearings and several trekking trails (500 m–5 km). :contentReference[oaicite:17]{index=17}

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Flying squirrel, slender loris, Indian pangolin, mouse deer, four‑horned antelope, Malabar tree toad, Malabar trogon, heart‑spotted woodpecker. :contentReference[oaicite:18]{index=18}
  • Flora: Tall native trees up to 30 m high; diverse moist‑deciduous flora. :contentReference[oaicite:19]{index=19}

Activities

  • Trekking trails, birdwatching, canopy watchtower sightings, tribal culture interaction. :contentReference[oaicite:20]{index=20}

Accessibility & Permits

Open year-round; access regulated via eco‑tourism facility; guided visits available. :contentReference[oaicite:21]{index=21}

Significance

A key sanctuary for Western Ghats wildlife; engages local tribal communities in eco-tourism. :contentReference[oaicite:22]{index=22}
