White Cliff Island Wildlife Sanctuary

Location & Area: Established in 1987, covering approximately 0.47 km² as part of Andaman reef-island sanctuaries :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}.

Geography & Habitat

Reef-fringed island featuring limestone cliffs, sandy shores and limited scrub vegetation inland.

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Seabird nesting zones, reef fish and small marine invertebrates.
  • Flora: Coastal shrubs, reef algae and seagrass patches.

Activities

  • Bird monitoring and coral biodiversity studies (by permit only).

Accessibility & Permits

Entry allowed only for authorized research missions under forest dept. Permits required.

Significance

Protects a reef-islet with unique limestone cliff structures supporting avifauna and coral biodiversity.
