Battimalv Island Wildlife Sanctuary

Location & Area: Situated about 30 km south of Car Nicobar in the Nicobar group; area ~2.23 km², established in 1987. Key site for coastal biodiversity and seabird nesting

Geography & Habitat

A flat cliff‑fringe islet with a single landing point; dense coconut and arrack nut plantations around a lighthouse; supports reef and tidal ecosystems

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Seabirds, possibly turtles and reef fish around the island.
  • Flora: Coconut and Arrack‑nut plantations are planted; sparse native vegetation

Activities

  • Birdwatching and nature observation
  • Photography of coastal and reef habitats

Accessibility & Permits

Accessible via boat from Car Nicobar; visits restricted—permits from forest department required.

Significance

Small island sanctuary recognized for its unique ecological character and seabird nesting habitat.
