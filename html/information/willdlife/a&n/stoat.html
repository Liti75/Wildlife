Stoat Island Wildlife Sanctuary

Location & Area: Located in North Andaman, established in 1987 and covering ~0.44 km² :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.

Geography & Habitat

A small reef‑islet with sandy shores and coastal shrub vegetation. Supports adjacent marine ecosystems and seabird nesting zones.

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Reef fish, seabirds, and occasional marine turtles.
  • Flora: Seagrasses, coral formations, littoral shrubs.

Activities

  • Marine biodiversity research
  • Bird nesting studies and reef surveys

Accessibility & Permits

Strictly regulated; only authorized researchers may enter with prior permission.

Significance

Helps conserve coral‑reef ecology and seabird habitat on a small island refuge in Andaman.
