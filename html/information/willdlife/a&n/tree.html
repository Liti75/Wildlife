Tree Island Wildlife Sanctuary

Location & Area: A tiny reef island of ~0.03 km² in North Andaman, declared in 1987.

Geography & Habitat

Low-lying reef islet with minimal vegetation and coral patches.

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Nesting seabirds, reef invertebrates and juvenile turtle reefs.
  • Flora: Sparse seagrasses and reef-edge algae.

Activities

  • Bird nesting observation and reef ecological surveys (by permit)

Accessibility & Permits

Entry restricted to researchers with forest department approval.

Significance

Helps conserve a unique microhabitat within Andaman’s reef‑island sanctuary system.
