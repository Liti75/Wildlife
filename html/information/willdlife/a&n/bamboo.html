Bamboo Island Wildlife Sanctuary

Location & Area: Located in North & Middle Andaman District, ~0.05 km². Established 1987 to protect bamboo coastal habitat.

Geography & Habitat

Island sanctuary blending open sea, coral reefs, bamboo forest, and mangrove fringes.

Flora & Fauna

  • Flora: Coastal bamboo groves, mangroves, reef vegetation.
  • Fauna: Marine turtles, seabirds, reef fish, some mammals.

Activities

  • Snorkeling and coral viewing
  • Birdwatching and nature walks

Accessibility & Permits

Accessible by ferry or ship from Port Blair and nearby jetties; forest department permit required.

Significance

Small but ecologically valuable sanctuary preserving coastal bamboo and associated marine biodiversity.
