Bondoville Island Wildlife Sanctuary

Location & Area: Located near South Andaman; established 1987, area approx. 2.55 km².

Geography & Habitat

Small reef-island with coral fringes and sheltered beaches.

Flora & Fauna

  • Fauna: Seabirds, reef and marine species.
  • Flora: Coastal shrubs and reef-edge vegetation.

Activities

  • Birdwatching
  • Marine and reef observation

Accessibility & Permits

Access via boat from Port Blair; forest department permit required.

Significance

Important coastal sanctuary for reef-associated species in South Andaman.
